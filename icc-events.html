ICC Event Winners (2007–2025)

2007 T20 World Cup

View Stats

Winner: India (defeated Pakistan by 5 runs)

Final: India 157/5 vs. Pakistan 152 (Johannesburg)

Top Run-Scorer: Matthew Hayden (Australia) – 265 runs

Top Wicket-Taker: Umar Gul (Pakistan) – 13 wickets

Notable: First T20 World Cup; MS Dhoni’s debut as captain.

2009 T20 World Cup

View Stats

Winner: Pakistan (defeated Sri Lanka by 8 wickets)

Final: Sri Lanka 138/6 vs. Pakistan 139/2 (Lord’s)

Top Run-Scorer: Tillakaratne Dilshan (Sri Lanka) – 317 runs

Top Wicket-Taker: Umar Gul (Pakistan) – 13 wickets

Notable: Shahid Afridi named Player of the Match.

2010 T20 World Cup

View Stats

Winner: England (defeated Australia by 7 wickets)

Final: Australia 147/6 vs. England 148/3 (Bridgetown)

Top Run-Scorer: Mahela Jayawardene (Sri Lanka) – 302 runs

Top Wicket-Taker: Dirk Nannes (Australia) – 14 wickets

Notable: England’s first ICC T20 title.

2011 ODI World Cup

View Stats

Winner: India (defeated Sri Lanka by 6 wickets)

Final: Sri Lanka 274/6 vs. India 277/4 (Mumbai)

Top Run-Scorer: Tillakaratne Dilshan (Sri Lanka) – 500 runs

Top Wicket-Taker: Shahid Afridi (Pakistan)/Zaheer Khan (India) – 21 wickets

Notable: MS Dhoni’s iconic six sealed the win.

2012 T20 World Cup

View Stats

Winner: West Indies (defeated Sri Lanka by 36 runs)

Final: West Indies 137/6 vs. Sri Lanka 101 (Colombo)

Top Run-Scorer: Shane Watson (Australia) – 249 runs

Top Wicket-Taker: Ajantha Mendis (Sri Lanka) – 15 wickets

Notable: First host nation to reach the final.

2013 Champions Trophy

View Stats

Winner: India (defeated England by 5 runs)

Final: India 129/7 vs. England 124/8 (Birmingham, 20 overs)

Top Run-Scorer: Shikhar Dhawan (India) – 363 runs

Top Wicket-Taker: Ravindra Jadeja (India) – 12 wickets

Notable: MS Dhoni became the first captain to win all three ICC trophies.

2015 ODI World Cup

View Stats

2019 ODI World Cup

View Stats

Winner: England (defeated New Zealand via boundary countback)
