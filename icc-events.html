ICC Event Winners (2007–2025)

🏆

2003 ODI World Cup

View Stats

Winner: Australia (Won by 125 runs)

Final: India 234 vs. Australia (Ricky Ponting)

Top Run-Scorer: Ricky Ponting (Australia) - 140* (121 balls, 4 fours, 8 sixes)

Top Wicket-Taker: Brett Lee (Australia) - 2/31

Notable: Australia’s second straight title, unbeaten run.

🏆

2007 ODI World Cup

View Stats

Winner: Australia (Won by 53 runs, D/L method)

Final: Australia 281/4 (38 overs) vs. Sri Lanka 215/8

Top Run-Scorer: Adam Gilchrist (Australia) - 149 (104 balls, 13 fours, 8 sixes)

Top Wicket-Taker: Glenn McGrath (Australia) - 2/35, 26 wickets in tournament

Notable: Australia’s third consecutive title, unbeaten.

2007 T20 World Cup

View Stats

Winner: India (Defeated Pakistan by 5 runs)
