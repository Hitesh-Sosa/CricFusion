About Delhi Capitals

City: New Delhi

Home Ground: Arun Jaitley Stadium (Visakhapatnam for some matches)

Captain (2025): Axar Patel

Owners: GMR Group and JSW Group

Established: 2008 (as Delhi Daredevils, rebranded in 2019)

Delhi Capitals are known for nurturing young talent and a strong bowling attack, with a focus on dynamic T20 cricket.
