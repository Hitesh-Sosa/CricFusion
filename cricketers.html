Legends of Cricket

Sachin Tendulkar (India)

Role: Batsman
Achievements:
- 34,357 international runs
- 100 international centuries
- 2011 World Cup winner
- First to score ODI double-century

Ricky Ponting (Australia)

Role: Batsman
Achievements:
- 13,378 Test runs
- Two-time World Cup captain (2003, 2007)
- 71 international centuries
- Most Test wins as captain

Kumar Sangakkara (Sri Lanka)

Role: Wicketkeeper-Batsman
Achievements:
- 12,400 Test runs
- 38 Test centuries
- 2014 T20 World Cup winner
- 487 dismissals as keeper

Kapil Dev (India)

Role: All-rounder
Achievements:
- 1983 World Cup-winning captain
- 434 Test wickets
- 5,248 Test runs
- First Indian pace-bowling legend

Brian Lara (West Indies)

Role: Batsman
Achievements:
- Record 400* in a Test innings
- 11,953 Test runs
- 34 Test centuries
- 501* in first-class cricket

Shane Warne (Australia)

Role: Bowler
Achievements:
- 708 Test wickets
- Revived leg-spin bowling
- 2007 World Cup winner
- Iconic “Ball of the Century”

Don Bradman (Australia)

Role: Batsman
Achievements:
- Test average of 99.94
- 6,996 Test runs
- 29 Test centuries
- Greatest batsman ever

Viv Richards (West Indies)

Role: Batsman
Achievements:
- 8,540 Test runs
- 1975, 1979 World Cup winner
- Fastest Test century (56 balls)
- Intimidating presence

Muttiah Muralitharan (Sri Lanka)

Role: Bowler
Achievements:
- 800 Test wickets
- 534 ODI wickets
- Most wickets in international cricket
- 1996 World Cup winner

Wasim Akram (Pakistan)

Role: Bowler
Achievements:
- 414 ODI wickets
- 502 Test wickets
- Master of swing bowling
- 1992 World Cup hero

Virat Kohli (India)

Role: Batsman
Achievements:
- 50 ODI centuries
- Fastest to 13,000 ODI runs
- 2011 World Cup winner
- ICC Player of the Decade (2010s)

AB de Villiers (South Africa)

Role: Batsman
Achievements:
- Fastest ODI century (31 balls)
- 8,765 Test runs
- 360-degree batting innovator
- T20 league legend

Rahul Dravid (India)

Role: Batsman
Achievements:
- 13,288 Test runs
- 36 Test centuries
- Known as “The Wall”
- 2003, 2007 Test series hero

Glenn McGrath (Australia)

Role: Bowler
Achievements:
- 563 Test wickets
- 381 ODI wickets
- Three-time World Cup winner
- Lethal accuracy

MS Dhoni (India)

Role: Wicketkeeper-Batsman
Achievements:
- 2011 World Cup-winning captain
- 2007 T20 WC, 2013 CT winner
- 10,773 ODI runs
- Lightning stumpings
