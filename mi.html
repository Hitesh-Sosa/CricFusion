Mumbai Indians

Five-time winners with a storied legacy

About Mumbai Indians

City: Mumbai, Maharashtra

Home Ground: Wankhede Stadium

Captain (2025): Hardik Pandya

Owners: Reliance Industries

Established: 2008

Mumbai Indians, led by Rohit Sharma and now Hardik Pandya, are the joint-most successful IPL franchise.
